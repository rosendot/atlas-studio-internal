# Growth roadmap

## Pricing

**Standard rate:** $349/mo — no setup fee, cancel anytime.

**First-month discount:** $299 for month 1 when a new client comes in through direct outreach or community channels. Reverts to $349 from month 2.

> Do not tie discounts to reviews. Incentivized Google reviews violate Google's policies and risk suspension of the Business Profile.

---

## Referral program (tiered)

Existing clients earn a permanent rate reduction based on how many paying clients they successfully refer.

| Referrals sent | Monthly rate |
|----------------|-------------|
| 0 (standard)   | $349/mo     |
| 1              | $329/mo     |
| 3              | $299/mo     |
| 5              | $279/mo     |

**Rules:**
- A referral counts only when the referred business signs up and makes their first payment.
- Each tier unlocks a fresh 6-month window at that rate — it does not continue from where a previous tier left off. Example: a client on month 4 of their $329 window who hits 3 referrals immediately starts a new 6-month window at $299.
- After the 6-month window expires, the client returns to $349 unless they have hit the next threshold, in which case a new window at that rate begins.
- Thresholds are cumulative — 3 referrals total unlocks $299, 5 referrals total unlocks $279.
- Referring client must remain an active subscriber to keep any discounted rate.

**Blended rate assumptions (default):** 50% no referral · 25% at $329 · 15% at $299 · 10% at $279 → ~$329/mo average.

## Growth channels

### 1. Direct outreach
Walk into local restaurants, salons, and retail shops. Pull up their website on your phone in front of them and point out specific issues (slow load, broken mobile layout, outdated menu). Offer the $299 first month to close on the spot.

**Target:** 3–5 in-person visits per week early on.

### 2. Facebook & Reddit groups
Join local business owner Facebook groups and relevant subreddits (e.g. r/smallbusiness, r/entrepreneur, local city groups). Be genuinely helpful — answer questions, offer quick audits. Do not spam. Let the reputation build naturally.

**Target:** Active presence in 3–5 local Facebook groups. Occasional value posts, not pitches.

### 3. First-month discount ($299)
Use the discounted first month as a low-friction close — especially effective after a direct outreach conversation or a warm lead from a community group. Makes it easy for a hesitant business owner to say yes.

---

## What to avoid

- Do not incentivize Google reviews with discounts or any other reward — it violates Google's Terms of Service.
- Do not spam community groups with sales pitches — get banned and damages reputation.
- Avoid paid ads until there are enough case studies and margin to spend (target: 15+ clients).
